Hi! I'm Glen Nelson.


I'm an illustrator, web designer, and Dungeon Master from Dallas, Texas, but based in Ireland. I've got a passion for creating nerdy things, wether you need an awesome website or just want your D&D character brought to life.


I don't think stories are restricted to just books or the tabletop. Everyone has a story, but most aren't given the opportunity to tell it. My passion is to help people like myself tell their stories.


I've been illustrating since I was a kid. When I was a kid I drew cartoon monsters and created my own comics and characters. I count myself lucky that I haven't lost the love for creating silly drawings.
